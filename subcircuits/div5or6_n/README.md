Divide by 5 or 6, no output
===========================

This circuit performs a frequency division by 5, or 6 depending on the
configuration of the two DIP switches. The circuit either behaves as a single
div5 (jumpers in the upper/outer positions) or as a div3 followed by a div2
(jumpers in the lower/inner positions).
